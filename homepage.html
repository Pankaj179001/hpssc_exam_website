VISION & MISSION

VISION 1)Achieving excellence in education by maintaining high standards in teaching, shaping talents of students, building their character and imbibing in them a sense of civic and social responsibility.
2)Empowering students to become able, responsible citizens by imparting quality education and inculcating sound ethical values.
3)Providing best infrastructure comparable to international standards. MISSION -To create perfect learning environment.
-To provide necessary infrastructure (auditorium, a new science block, a new block for B. Voc., New block for Library).
-To provide clean and pollution free environment under ‘Swachh Bharat Abhiyan’.
-To provide academic facilities to the teachers.
-To encourage students to participate in extracurricular activities.
-To provide Skill Based Education.
-To improve communication skills of the students for personality enhancement.
-To focus on bringing the students at par with the students of metropolitan cities.
-To reach out to society through NCC, NSS, Rover and Rangers for implementation of govt. policies like Swachh Bharat Abhiyan .
-To start new courses BA in Tourism, B.Sc. In Computer Science from the session 2019-20.
